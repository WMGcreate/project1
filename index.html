DEPARTMENTS

our dedicated neurologists provide comprehensive care for a wide range of neurological conditions. Our experts specialize in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson's disease. We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic tools, such as advanced imaging techniques and neurophysiological tests, to accurately assess and manage neurological health.
our dermatology team offers expert care for a wide array of skin, hair, and nail conditions. Our experienced dermatologists provide comprehensive services for issues such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer. Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools and treatment methods, including skin biopsies and laser therapy, we deliver personalized care tailored to each patient's needs
our orthopedic specialists provide expert care for musculoskeletal conditions, including bone, joint, and muscle disorders. We offer comprehensive services for conditions such as fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, and spinal disorders. Our team utilizes advanced diagnostic techniques and cutting-edge treatments, including minimally invasive surgery and physical therapy, to restore mobility and improve quality of life. .
We provide expert diagnosis and treatment for issues such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension. Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools like echocardiograms and stress tests, along with cutting-edge therapies, we focus on both acute and preventive cardiology. Our patient-centered approach ensures personalized care plans aimed at improving heart health and enhancing overall well-being.
